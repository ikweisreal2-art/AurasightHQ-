PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: February 2026

1. DATA COLLECTION

AuraSightHQ processes live CCTV video streams. We do not store raw footage unless "Neural Archive" is enabled by the operator.

2. AI & NEURAL PROCESSING

Our AI analyzes metadata (motion, objects, timestamps). All processing is end-to-end encrypted. We do not sell biometric data to third parties.

3. SECURITY MEASURES

We use 256-bit encryption. Access to "Active Nodes" is restricted to verified operators only.
